# B1 · Vocabulario 04 — Pasatiempos y Tiempo Libre (Hobbies & Free Time)

## Actividades comunes

| Inglés | Español |
|---|---|
| Reading | Leer |
| Painting / Drawing | Pintar / Dibujar |
| Gardening | Jardinería |
| Cooking / Baking | Cocinar / Hornear |
| Photography | Fotografía |
| Hiking | Senderismo |
| Playing video games | Jugar videojuegos |
| Knitting | Tejer |

## Verbos + gerundio para hobbies (repaso)

> *I enjoy **reading** in my free time.*
> *She's really into **photography**.*
> *He's passionate about **cooking**.*

## Expresiones útiles

| Expresión | Significado |
|---|---|
| to be into something | estar interesado en algo |
| to be a big fan of | ser gran fan de |
| to take up a hobby | empezar un pasatiempo |
| to have a lot of free time | tener mucho tiempo libre |

## En contexto

> *I recently **took up** painting — it's really relaxing.*
> *What do you do in your free time?*
> *I'm not really into sports, but I love **hiking**.*

## Consejos de pronunciación

- **Hiking** /ˈhaɪkɪŋ/.
- **Knitting** /ˈnɪtɪŋ/.

## Práctica

Describe 3 pasatiempos tuyos usando "enjoy", "be into" y "take up".
